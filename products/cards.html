Connect Financial is the world’s first crypto credit card, powered by Visa. Simply fund a collateral wallet and take advantage of instant credit without needing to sell your digital assets.

Card Tiers How It Works

Credit Made Easy.

Finally, you can instantly receive a level of credit that meets your needs without the hassle of credit checks and slow application processes.

When you load assets including BTC, ETH and LTC onto your card as collateral, we will extend a line of credit to you based on a portion of its total value. The more you load, the more credit and card benefits you receive, significantly increasing your purchase power.

Pay Worldwide

Enjoy your crypto credit card globally in 200 countries at 46+ million merchants through the elite Visa network.

Fast & Easy

Simply sign up, select a card and fund your collateral wallet. No credit checks or lengthy application processes.

Payment Freedom

Settle on your terms with flexible payment options, low minimum payment amounts and interest as low as 7.90%.

Take Control

Manage your transactions, cards and payments through a powerful dashboard, giving you full control in your account.

The World's First True Crypto Credit Card.

When you use a Connect Financial Visa card, you spend our credit, not your crypto. Maintain continued appreciation of your digital assets while taking advantage of a true line of credit with the flexibility and worldwide acceptance of the Visa network.

Unlike other crypto cards, we don't force you to sell your assets to make a purchase. When it comes time to settle your bill, pay it on your terms - with crypto or by bank transfer, ACH or wire.
