We empower people and businesses to do more with their digital assets. We don't think you should have to choose between spending today and your future financial goals.

Be In Control, At Every Step.

Our Beginning

Connect Financial was founded by a small, passionate team with decades of experience in the financial industry. As well as being corporate veterans, we are also cryptocurrency pioneers, driving innovation in the industry since 2013.

We recognized the need for crypto to advance from just an investment vehicle to leverage its value in daily lives. Bringing the world's first true crypto credit card to market is just one of the innovative products in our product ecosystem.

Our Mission

We aim to transform the way that people think about crypto and manage their financial assets.

We help unlock the value of cryptocurrency without destroying its underlying value. We bridge the gap between DeFi and traditional finance by allowing people to spend, grow, transact and securely store their fiat and digital assets. Allowing people around the globe to easily connect the benefits of their crypto investments to their world and everyday lives.

Our Values

At Connect Financial, we are driven by divine discontent and a relentless pursuit of excellence. We aspire to reach highest levels of integrity in everything that we do, and we know that the trust of our users underpins our success.

We're a flexible, global and remote team of professionals from a range of backgrounds, cultures and worldviews. Nurturing curiosity and recognising that great ideas can truly come from anyone, regardless of their role is in our core foundational DNA.

Building The Future Of Crypto.

With the rapid growth in our online eco-system we believe everyone should:
  • Have full control of their crypto, money and investments.
  • Not be limited to money vs digital assets.
  • Have security and control of their personal data and identity online.
  • Have worldwide financial flexibility.
